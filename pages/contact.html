Have Questions?

Common questions about admissions, campus visits, and more.

Applications are submitted through the Parcoursup platform for Bachelor's programs (French students), and directly via EFREI's online application portal for international and Master's students. Deadlines are typically in February–March for the following academic year.
Yes. EFREI participates in the national scholarship system (CROUS), and the school also offers merit-based grants and social assistance funds. Additional funding is available through apprenticeship contracts, where your employer pays your tuition.
